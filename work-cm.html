Cooking Matters

Re-design project of NPO website “Cooking Matters”. We focused on recipes and educational tools.

Overview
Team:
Barbara Soares, Maggie Ren, Nattalie Dai, Philip Dural, and Ryoko Hughes
The Client:
Cooking Matters is helping to end childhood hunger by inspiring and educating families to make healthy and affordable food choices.
User Insight:
Our users need to find resources for efficient and economical recipes with relevant nutritional information to meet their needs.
